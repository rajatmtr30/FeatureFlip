How to Test on Mobile

  • Portrait Upright: Hold your phone normally (home button at bottom)
  • Landscape Right: Rotate phone 90° clockwise (home button on right)
  • Portrait Upside Down: Flip phone upside down (home button at top)
  • Landscape Left: Rotate phone 90° counter-clockwise (home button on left)

Note: Some devices may not support all orientations. The app will automatically detect the best available orientation.
